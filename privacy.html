Privacy Policy

App: LyricsFlow
Last updated: May 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LyricsFlow ("we", "us") handles information when you use the LyricsFlow app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Audio files — uploaded by you for speech-to-text transcription. Files are processed on our server and are not stored permanently after processing is complete.
  • Usage data — anonymous usage statistics (number of transcriptions, app version). No personal identifiers are collected.
  • Purchase data — handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process audio and return word-level timestamps to your device.
  • To manage your credit balance stored locally on your device.
  • To display relevant ads via Google AdMob (if you are not a VIP subscriber).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — used to show rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data per their own Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — used for in-app purchases and subscriptions.

4. Data Storage

Your tracks and word edits are stored locally on your device only. Audio files uploaded for transcription are deleted from our server after processing. We do not maintain a user account system and do not store personal data on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

7. Contact

Questions? Contact us at: support@lyricsflow.info
